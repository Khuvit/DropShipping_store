Product Details

The 12.9-inch iPad Pro (6th generation) was introduced in 2022. It features a Liquid Retina XDR display with a resolution of 2732-by-2048 pixels and a contrast ratio of 1,000,000:. The display has rounded corners that follow a beautiful curved design, and these corners are within a standard rectangle. When measured as a standard rectangular shape, the screen is 12.9 inches diagonally. The 12.9-inch iPad Pro is available in two finishes: Silver and Space Gray. It comes in five storage capacities: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, and 2TB1. The Wi-Fi model weighs 1.5 pounds (682 grams), while the Wi-Fi + Cellular model weighs 1.51 pounds (685 grams).
