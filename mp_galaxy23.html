Product Details

The Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra is the latest addition to the Galaxy S series. It features a 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with a 120Hz refresh rate and a resolution of 3088 x 1440 pixels1. The device is powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor and has up to 12GB of RAM and 1TB of storage. One of the standout features of the Galaxy S23 Ultra is its camera system. It has a whopping 200MP wide-angle camera, as well as a 12MP ultra-wide camera, and two telephoto cameras with 3x and 10x optical zoom. The device also has a built-in S Pen, which allows you to take notes, draw, and control the camera remotely. The Galaxy S23 Ultra is available in several colors, including Green, Lavender, and Cream
